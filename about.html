Our Roots

A Family Recipe,
A Highway Legend

Juno started as a small roadside bakery in Tangutur — the kind of place where a travelling family could stop, stretch their legs, sip the best chai they'd ever had, and pick up something freshly baked for the road ahead. That spirit has never changed.

Today, we serve thousands of travellers every month — from families on road trips to truckers, students, and curious wanderers who found us on Google and couldn't resist the 4.1 stars. We think they come back because the food tastes like it was made with someone in mind. Because it was.

Our Journey

50 Years of Baking

1975

Founded on NH-16

Juno opens its doors as a small bakery stall on the Chennai–Kolkata National Highway near Tangutur, serving fresh buns and chai to early morning travellers.

1990

Irani Chai Becomes Our Signature

After perfecting the recipe over 15 years, our Irani Dum Chai becomes what regulars call the best chai on any AP highway. Word spreads fast.

2005

Full Bakery & Restaurant Opens

We expand the space to include a full restaurant wing — separate vegetarian and non-vegetarian sections, clean restrooms, and a dedicated cake counter.

2020

1,000+ Reviews on Google

Juno earns over 1,000 Google reviews with a strong 4.1-star rating. Travellers from all over India leave behind their stories of that one chai that made the trip.

Now

Still Baking. Still Brewing.

With over 1,437 reviews and a loyal community of travellers, Juno continues to be the highway's most beloved pitstop — one fresh bun at a time.
